Job Description for Game Testers (f/m/d)

As a Game Tester, you will be responsible for playing and testing games to identify bugs, glitches, and other issues. Your role will involve:

  • Playing through game content to identify issues
  • Reporting and documenting bugs and errors
  • Collaborating with the development team to ensure issues are resolved
  • Verifying fixes and improvements
  • Providing feedback on game features and usability

Requirements:

  • Passion for gaming and a keen eye for detail
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Fluency in German and English
